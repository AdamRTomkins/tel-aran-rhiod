--- layout: null section-type: about title: About --- ## About Tel'aran'rhiod is literary creation of Robert Jordan, author of the exceptional Wheel of Time fantasy series. Tel'aran'rhiod, or the World of Dreams is described as a world that is both of the world and apart from it, possibly lying between the weaves of the Great Pattern. Most people cannot enter it deliberately, though many touch it briefly, for just a few moments. I have a created this simple app to test out the ability of Threejs to create virtual reality sites for google cardboard. Create your own world of dreams by starring or forking it!
